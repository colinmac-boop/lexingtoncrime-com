About Tates Creek

Tates Creek is one of Lexington's most well-known and expansive neighborhoods, stretching along Tates Creek Road in south Lexington. The area encompasses several sub-neighborhoods and is anchored by Tates Creek High School, one of Fayette County's premier public high schools. Housing ranges from established 1970s-era homes to newer construction, with many properties offering generous lots and mature landscaping. The Tates Creek corridor provides access to shopping, dining, and parks, making it one of the most desirable family neighborhoods in the city.

Safety Tips for Tates Creek

🏫

School Zone Awareness

During school hours, drive carefully through the neighborhood. Watch for students walking and biking, especially near Tates Creek High School.

🏠

Neighborhood Watch

With a large, spread-out neighborhood, organized watch groups help cover more ground. Coordinate with your nearest neighbors.

🚗

Driveway Security

Don't leave cars unlocked in driveways, even in quiet sub-neighborhoods. Most vehicle break-ins target unlocked doors.
