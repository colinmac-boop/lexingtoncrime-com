About Zandale

Zandale is a small, established neighborhood in south Lexington centered around the Zandale Shopping Center on Nicholasville Road. The area features modest single-family homes and apartments from the 1960s and 1970s, offering some of the most affordable housing options close to UK campus and Fayette Mall. Zandale's central south Lexington location provides walkable access to retail, restaurants, and public transit along the Nicholasville Road corridor. The neighborhood attracts UK students, young professionals, and long-time residents who value convenience and affordability.

Safety Tips for Zandale

🚶

Walkway Awareness

Nicholasville Road is a busy corridor. Use designated crosswalks, especially at night, and stay aware of turning vehicles near shopping centers.

🚗

Vehicle Security

In mixed residential-commercial areas, vehicle break-ins happen. Lock doors, roll up windows, and keep valuables out of sight.

🔒

Apartment Safety

If renting, test all locks before moving in. Request repairs for any security issues — landlords are required to maintain safe entry points.
