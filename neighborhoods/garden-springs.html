About Garden Springs

Garden Springs is a quiet residential neighborhood in west Lexington, situated between Versailles Road and Alexandria Drive. The area features modest single-family homes built primarily in the 1960s and 1970s, many with spacious backyards and mature landscaping. Garden Springs Elementary School serves as a neighborhood anchor, and the area is known for its tight-knit community atmosphere. Residents enjoy convenient access to Masterson Station Park and the growing commercial corridor along Versailles Road.

Safety Tips for Garden Springs

🌳

Yard Maintenance

Keep hedges and bushes trimmed below window level. Overgrown landscaping can provide hiding spots for intruders.

🏠

Home Visibility

Ensure your home is visible from the street. Good sightlines between neighbors help everyone watch out for each other.

💡

Porch Lighting

Keep porch lights on from dusk to dawn. A well-lit front entrance is a simple but effective deterrent.
