About LexingtonCrime.com

LexingtonCrime.com is your go-to resource for crime data, neighborhood safety guides, and community safety information in Lexington, Kentucky.

Our Mission

We believe informed communities are safer communities. By making crime data accessible and easy to understand, we help Lexington residents and visitors make better decisions about where to live, work, and visit.

Our Data

Crime data on LexingtonCrime.com is sourced from the Lexington Police Department and aggregated through SpotCrime, one of the largest and most trusted crime data platforms in the United States. Data is updated daily to give you the most current picture of crime in Lexington.

Neighborhood Guides

Our neighborhood safety guides go beyond raw data. Each guide includes local context, safety tips specific to the area, and information about what makes each neighborhood unique. Whether you're considering a move or just want to understand your community better, our guides are here to help.

Crime Alerts

Sign up for free email alerts to receive notifications about crime in your area. Stay informed without having to check the map every day — we'll bring the information to you.

Have questions, feedback, or want to partner with us? Reach out at info@lexingtoncrime.com.
