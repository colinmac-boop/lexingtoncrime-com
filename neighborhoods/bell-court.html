About Bell Court

Bell Court is a charming historic neighborhood located just southeast of downtown Lexington, bounded roughly by East Main Street, South Limestone, and the railroad corridor. Designated as a Preserve America Community, the neighborhood is recognized for its commitment to preserving its architectural heritage and fostering community engagement with local history.

The neighborhood features a mix of early 20th-century housing styles, including Craftsman bungalows, shotgun houses, and Four-Square homes, many of which have been lovingly restored by residents. Bell Court's compact, walkable streets create a village-like feel that's rare in a city Lexington's size. The active Bell Court Neighborhood Association organizes events, maintains common spaces, and advocates for the community's interests.

With its proximity to downtown, Transylvania University, and Gratz Park, Bell Court offers residents an ideal blend of historic character, walkability, and urban convenience. Its tight-knit community and distinctive sense of place make it one of Lexington's most beloved small neighborhoods.

Bell Court hosts annual community events including porch concerts, neighborhood yard sales, and holiday gatherings that reflect its strong community bonds. The area's compact grid layout makes it one of the most bikeable neighborhoods in Lexington, with easy connections to the Legacy Trail and downtown bike lanes. Many homes in the neighborhood have been thoughtfully renovated, preserving original hardwood floors, built-in cabinetry, and period details while incorporating modern amenities.

Safety Tips for Bell Court

🏘️

Know Your Neighbors

Bell Court's tight-knit community is its greatest safety asset. Join the neighborhood association, attend events, and keep an eye out for each other — community vigilance is powerful.

🚲

Bike & Porch Security

The neighborhood's walkability and front-porch culture are wonderful — but secure bikes, furniture, and packages. Use sturdy locks and bring valuables inside at night.

🔑

Door & Window Locks

Many Bell Court homes have original hardware. Ensure all entry points have functional deadbolts and window locks. Smart locks can add convenience without compromising historic character.
