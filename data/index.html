Kentucky at a glance, 2025 (FBI data)

Kentucky's violent crime rate was 198.5 per 100,000 residents, roughly 39% below the U.S. rate of 325.2. The state's murder rate (2.7 per 100,000) was below the U.S. figure of 4.3; robbery (28.0 vs. 48.7) and aggravated assault (136.1 vs. 235.9) also ran well below national norms.
