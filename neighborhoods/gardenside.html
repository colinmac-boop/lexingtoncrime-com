About Gardenside

Gardenside is a well-established neighborhood in south Lexington, located along Nicholasville Road near Fayette Mall. The area features a mix of ranch-style homes from the 1960s and 1970s alongside newer townhouse developments. Gardenside is anchored by its own shopping center and is walkable to numerous restaurants and retail options along the Nicholasville Road corridor. The neighborhood is popular with UK students and young professionals due to its proximity to the University of Kentucky campus and affordable rents.

Safety Tips for Gardenside

Walking Safety

Nicholasville Road is a busy corridor. Stay on sidewalks, use crosswalks, and stay alert when walking to nearby shopping areas, especially at night.

🔒

Rental Security

If renting, ensure landlords maintain quality locks and exterior lighting. Report any security concerns promptly.
