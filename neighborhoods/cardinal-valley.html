About Cardinal Valley

Cardinal Valley is a diverse, affordable neighborhood in southwest Lexington along Versailles Road. The area is anchored by Cardinal Valley Elementary School and features a mix of single-family homes, apartments, and townhouse communities. Known for its multicultural character, the neighborhood reflects Lexington's growing diversity with restaurants and shops representing cuisines from around the world along Versailles Road. The community benefits from nearby Jacobson Park and easy access to Bluegrass Airport.
