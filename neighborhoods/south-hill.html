About South Hill

South Hill is a historic residential neighborhood located just south of downtown Lexington, known for its charming older homes and walkable streets. The area features a mix of architectural styles from the early to mid-20th century, including Colonial Revival, Craftsman, and Cape Cod homes. South Hill's proximity to downtown, UK campus, and the Woodland Triangle area makes it popular with professionals and university staff. The neighborhood has a quiet, residential feel despite being just minutes from the city's urban core, with tree-canopied streets and well-maintained properties.
