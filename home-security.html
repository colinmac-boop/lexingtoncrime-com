Home Security Companies in Lexington

Three locally-operating security companies covering the Lexington and Central Kentucky market — residential alarms, cameras, monitoring, and smart-home integration.

Questions worth asking before you sign

  • What is the actual monthly monitoring cost after introductory pricing ends?
  • Is there a contract term, and what is the cancellation fee?
  • Who owns the equipment — you, or the company? What happens if you switch providers?
  • Where is the monitoring center, and is it UL-certified?
  • How is the system connected — cellular, broadband, both? What happens during an outage?
  • Will police actually respond? Most jurisdictions require permits and verified alarms; ask how false-alarm fees are handled.
  • Get the license number — Kentucky regulates alarm installers; confirm the company holds an active state license.
