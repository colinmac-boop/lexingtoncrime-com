Safety Tips for Lexington

Practical advice to help keep you, your family, and your property safe in Lexington, Kentucky.

🏠

Home Security

  • • Lock all doors and windows, even when home. Most residential burglaries happen through unlocked entry points.
  • • Install exterior lighting with motion sensors around all entry points.
  • • Consider a video doorbell or security camera system. Even visible dummy cameras deter some burglars.
  • • Don't advertise vacations on social media. Have a trusted neighbor collect mail and packages.
  • • Keep shrubs trimmed near windows and doors so they can't provide concealment.
  • • Get to know your neighbors. A connected community is a safer community.
🚗

Vehicle Safety

  • • Never leave valuables visible in your car — bags, electronics, even loose change attracts thieves.
  • • Always lock your car and take your keys. Vehicle theft from unlocked cars is extremely common.
  • • Park in well-lit areas, especially at shopping centers and entertainment venues.
  • • Don't leave your car running unattended, even briefly ("puffing"). It's an invitation for theft.
  • • Consider a steering wheel lock or catalytic converter shield, especially for SUVs and trucks.
🚶

Personal Safety

  • • Stay aware of your surroundings. Avoid walking while distracted by your phone.
  • • Walk confidently and stay in well-lit, populated areas at night.
  • • Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, move to a safe location or call 911.
  • • Don't flash expensive jewelry, electronics, or cash in public.
  • • Share your location with a trusted contact when going out, especially at night.
💻

Fraud & Scam Prevention

  • • Never give personal information to unsolicited callers, emailers, or door-to-door visitors.
  • • Verify requests from "banks" or "government agencies" by calling the official number directly.
  • • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.
  • • Be cautious with online marketplace transactions. Meet in public, well-lit locations — Lexington PD offers safe exchange zones.
  • • Monitor your credit reports regularly for unauthorized activity.
