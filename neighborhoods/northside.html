About Northside

Northside is one of Lexington's most historically significant African American neighborhoods, located north of downtown along North Broadway and North Limestone. The area has deep cultural roots, including connections to the city's civil rights history and historically Black institutions. Northside features a mix of older residential homes, community centers, and churches that serve as social anchors. In recent years, the neighborhood has seen both revitalization investment and concerns about gentrification, with long-time residents working to preserve the area's cultural identity while welcoming positive change.
